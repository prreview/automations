## product notes

AlemHealth connects hospitals and diagnostic centres in emerging markets to a global network of diagnostic imaging services. Our suite of plug-and-play radiology solutions makes it easy for any facility anywhere to access and provide world-class diagnostic care to its patients. We designed our hardware and software from the ground up specifically for emerging markets, where access to internet, electricity, and clinicians can be difficult. Learn more about our solutions at www.alemhealth.com.
