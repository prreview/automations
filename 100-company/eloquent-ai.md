### Public job text
## **Meet Eloquent AI**

At Eloquent AI, we’re building the next generation of AI Operators—multimodal, autonomous systems that execute complex workflows across fragmented tools with human-level precision. Our technology goes far beyond chat: it sees, reads, clicks, types, and makes decisions—transforming how work gets done in regulated, high-stakes environments.

We’re already powering some of the world’s leading financial institutions and insurers, fundamentally changing how millions of people manage their finances every day. From automating compliance reviews to handling customer operations, our Operators are quietly replacing repetitive, manual tasks with intelligent, end-to-end execution.

Headquartered in San Francisco with a global footprint, Eloquent AI is a fast-growing company backed by top-tier investors. Join us to work alongside world-class talent in AI, engineering, and product as we redefine the future of financial services.

**Your Role**

As a Front-End Engineer at Eloquent AI, you’ll play a central role in shaping the interfaces that connect humans and AI in high-stakes enterprise environments. You’ll work closely with our product designers, AI engineers, and backend developers to build clean, fast, and intuitive UIs for our AI agents and dashboards.

This role is ideal for someone who thrives in fast-moving, design-conscious environments, is excited by the challenge of creating seamless user experiences for complex AI workflows, and who brings technical excellence and a deep attention to detail.

**You will:**

- Build beautiful, performant UIs that make AI-powered tools intuitive and powerful for enterprise users.

- Work on CUI (Conversational User Interface) design, agent dashboards, and visualisation tools that communicate complex AI outputs clearly.

- Collaborate cross-functionally with design, backend, and ML teams to turn product ideas into polished, functional front-end implementations.

- Create modular, scalable components with a strong foundation in design systems and accessibility.

- Optimise for performance and responsiveness across browsers and devices.

- Continuously iterate on design and UX through testing, feedback, and usage data.

**Requirements:**

- 3+ years of experience in front-end development, preferably building SaaS or AI-powered applications.

- Proficiency in React, TypeScript, HTML, and CSS.

- Experience working with design systems (e.g., Storybook, Figma handoff, Tailwind, or similar).
