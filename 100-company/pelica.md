### Public job text
## About Us

Pelica Health is the [operating system for value-based care](https://www.pelica.com/blog/ai-agents-vs-analytics-dashboards). We unify claims, EHR, pharmacy, lab, and ADT data into one live record per member, then put an AI copilot next to every team that depends on it, across [risk adjustment](https://www.pelica.com/blog/best-risk-adjustment-software), [Quality and Stars](https://www.pelica.com/blog/2027-medicare-star-ratings-changes), [pharmacy and Part D](https://www.pelica.com/blog/part-d-adherence-measures-explained), provider network, and [care management](https://www.pelica.com/blog/transitions-of-care-adt-automation).

**Read our founder letter:** [Why we started Pelica Health](https://www.pelica.com/blog/why-we-started-pelica).

Pelica was founded by former engineering and AI leaders from Google and YouTube, including co-founders who built large-scale infrastructure and machine learning systems. You will work alongside people who built massive systems at scale, a chance to learn a lot and contribute meaningfully from day one. We are backed by Y Combinator.

We believe in solving hard problems together as a team, iterating quickly, and building software with long-term thinking and ownership.

## What You'll Do

* Work full stack: design and build features spanning front end, back end, data storage, and processing.
* Build new product modules and services from scratch, or evolve existing ones, guided by real healthcare data and workflows.
* Work with AI and machine learning: integrate large language models, process large clinical and claims datasets, apply traditional ML, and build tooling around AI-driven flows.
* Make architectural decisions across frameworks, data models, APIs, and storage, balancing performance, scalability, maintainability, and complexity.
* Collaborate closely with co-founders and engineers to turn product vision into a working, maintainable codebase.

## What We're Looking For

* At least 3 years of full-stack engineering experience, including substantial work with AI/ML.
* Strong skills across front end, back end, and databases, with a demonstrated ability to design end-to-end systems.
* Experience integrating AI/ML: LLMs, data pipelines, long-form text processing, and traditional ML.
* Good design sense and architectural thinking. You understand trade-offs and choose wisely based on constraints.
* Comfort in a fast-paced startup environment: nimble, iterative, high ownership.
* Bonus: prior startup or founder…
