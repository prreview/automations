### Public job text
### Why you should consider joining:

About [**Reacher**](https://www.ycombinator.com/companies/reacher)**:** We're the #1 TikTok Shop partner helping brands like Under Armour, Hanes, HeyDude, and Logitech scale their affiliate marketing. We've crossed 7 figures in ARR and are looking to raise a seed round and double our team by this year.

Our vision is to become the Hubspot for creator marketing, powering brands and creators to connect and grow across all commerce platforms like Youtube Shopping, Instagram Shopping, Shopify, and Amazon. Reacher will be a key infrastructure component of the creator economy.

Personally, I find the space and our company exciting because we have the opportunity to implement AI for the creator economy with the largest brands and creators in the world.

**Your work will directly impact users on day 1.** We have a highly responsive user base that depends on our product day in and day out.

### What you'll do:

* Own and lead product features end-to-end: research, design, implement, test, and ship.
* Talk directly to customers and translate vague requirements into polished experiences.
* Design scalable backend systems (we use Python, FastAPI, PostgreSQL, GCP).
* Build responsive, delightful UIs (we use React, Tailwind, shadcn/ui).
* Write clean, maintainable, and testable code with minimal oversight.
* Contribute to architectural decisions and engineering processes.



**Work with a high sense of urgency – we ship fast and learn fast.**



### You're a fit if:

* You've got 1–3 years of professional experience, ideally at a product-first company or FAANG-level environment (SDE-2 level ownership expected).
* You're comfortable building full-stack features — from schema design to frontend polish.
* You care about product, not just code.
* You've owned something meaningful end-to-end in the past (a product module, an infra refactor, etc).
* You're resourceful and comfortable with ambiguity. You figure things out.



### Bonus points if:

* You've worked in an early-stage startup or been a first hire before.
* You've built internal tooling, dashboards, or systems that scaled to thousands of users.
* You're curious about AI, e-commerce, or automation — we work in that space.
* You've shipped side projects or contributed to open-source.



### Why join us:

* We're post-revenue and solving real problems for real customers.
* You'll get autonomy, speed, and visibility — no boring tickets.
* Your code will be in front of users…
