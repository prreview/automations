# Company: Fathom AI
- **Description**: Fathom is an AI notetaker that records, transcribes, highlights, and summarizes video calls across Zoom, Microsoft Teams, and Google Meet. Founded by Richard White, the company participated in Y Combinator (W21). They recently released Fathom 3.0 and hold the #1 position on the HubSpot app marketplace. Fathom's revenue model relies on premium subscriptions for advanced AI features and team-level CRM integrations. The startup is well-funded, having raised $17M Series A in January 2024 to push their runway and fuel rapid expansion of both their AI features and engineering teams.
