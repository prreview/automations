# Company: Hotwash
- **Description**: Hotwash is an after-action review platform designed specifically for fire departments. After a serious call, fire crews record the events, and the platform transcribes and structures these post-call reviews using Groq/AssemblyAI and Gemini to preserve institutional memory. The company is generating $40K ARR with 11 paying fire departments and zero churn, and is currently raising a $1M pre-seed round.
