# Company: Veedma
- **Description**: Veedma is a direct-to-consumer telehealth practice focused on men's hormonal and sexual health. They treat low testosterone, ED, and vitamin D deficiency through a cash-pay subscription model that includes online consultations, bi-annual lab panels, prescribed medications (exclusively Enclomiphene-only), and unlimited messaging. The startup is founder-led by an MD urologist acting as CEO and a technical CTO, focusing entirely on cash-pay clinical care without relying on traditional testosterone replacement therapy.
