# Company: Kairos Sports Research
- **Description**: Kairos Sports Research is a Paris-based sports forecasting shop. The company builds large-scale AI systems that predict sports outcomes, turns injury reports and game context into forecasts, and uses those forecasts to trade on prediction markets and sell odds and forecast feeds to sportsbooks. The public hiring post says the team was 7 people when the role went live, so this is a tiny crew with real ownership. Public funding is not disclosed. Revenue comes from prediction-market trading and forecast sales; runway is not public, but the business already has 2 ways to make money.
