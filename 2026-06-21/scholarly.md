# Company: Scholarly
- **Description**: Scholarly builds faculty software for higher education. The product is the system of record for faculty data, with workflows for annual reviews, promotion and tenure, appointment tracking, leave, and admin work. It sells B2B SaaS to colleges and universities, with onboarding and support wrapped around institution-level contracts. The company says it is backed by software investors, is seed stage, and LinkedIn shows 25 employees, founded in 2023, with teams in Denver and Seattle. Runway is not public.
