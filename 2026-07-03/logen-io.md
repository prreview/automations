# Company: Logen.io (LotusX Technologies Private Limited)
- **Description**: Logen.io is an AI app builder that turns plain-English descriptions into production-ready full-stack web apps. Users describe their idea, and Logen's "logic engine" builds the interface, backend logic, auth, PostgreSQL database, and deploys it to a live hosted URL in under 3 minutes. Think Lovable/Bolt/v0 but targeting the Indian market with real deployable apps, not just prototypes. The product is in public beta, with a freemium model (free tier with 1 preview app, $3/mo personal tier with unlimited apps, $12/mo pro tier). Apps get isolated infrastructure with automatic SSL, health monitoring, and custom domain support. The company is registered as LotusX Technologies Private Limited in Bengaluru. They've built a community of demo apps already shipped through the platform (real estate listings, event booking, ecommerce, project management, games). Very early stage, likely pre-seed or bootstrapped, with a small team. The tech stack includes Elixir/Phoenix on the backend, React for frontend, and deployment infrastructure for generated apps. This is a codegen platform at its core, which directly overlaps with Raj's WaveMaker experience building a transpiler that converts proprietary markup into production Next.js code.
