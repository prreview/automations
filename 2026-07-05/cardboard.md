# Company: Cardboard
- **Description**: Cardboard is an agentic video editor that lives in your browser. It gives you the power of Premiere Pro or DaVinci Resolve without the learning curve. Users go from raw footage (talking heads, screen recordings, B-rolls) to a finished video in seconds. The product watches your footage for you, finds the best moments, reframes videos into vertical/square/landscape instantly, and lets teams collaborate in real time with timecode-precise comments. Founded by childhood friends Saksham Aggarwal and Ishan Sharma, who built the product from their own pain: Ishan was producing Apple-style internal launch videos for HackerRank's all-hands demo days (350+ attendees). The company is in YC's Winter 2026 batch, headquartered in Bengaluru, with a team of 4. Revenue model is SaaS subscription targeting growth/marketing teams that use video as a distribution channel. Very early stage (pre-seed, YC-backed). Tech stack: Web-based (likely Next.js/React given the frontend-heavy role), WebGPU for rendering, AI/LLM-powered video understanding and editing.
