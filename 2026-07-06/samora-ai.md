# Company: Samora AI

- **Description**: Samora AI builds multilingual voice agents for high-volume calling operations. They automate inbound and outbound calls with AI agents that handle dialects, code-switching, and local conversation patterns, then safely escalate to humans when calls get sensitive. Customers include UNICEF, Signify, and the government of India. They have processed over 1 million calls. Built as a managed platform: teams describe call flows in plain language and Samora handles agent design, testing, integrations, deployment, monitoring, and escalation. They charge for managed voice operations, so revenue scales with call volume and deployment complexity. Backed by YC (W26 batch). Scaled to $50K MRR within months of launch, targeting $100K MRR. Team of 8, based in Bengaluru. Closing a seed round. Competitors include Retell AI, Vapi, Bland AI, but Samora differentiates on multilingual capability (critical for Indian and emerging markets), managed deployment (not just API), and strict safety controls for regulated industries.
