# Company: Logen.io
- **Description**: Logen.io is a Bengaluru-based startup building an AI app builder for full-stack web applications. The product lets users describe an app in plain English, then generates, hosts, and deploys a real full-stack app with auth, PostgreSQL database, backend logic, custom domains, and a live URL. The pipeline is three moves: Describe (natural language input), Build (logic engine architects the full stack), and Deploy (launched on isolated, enterprise-grade infrastructure with gVisor containers, automatic SSL, health monitoring). Now in public beta. The generated apps include interface, database schema, auth, business logic, and editable app structure. Revenue model is infrastructure-based: free tier to start, paid plans for higher container limits, with enterprise plans offering dedicated VPS, SSO (SAML 2.0/OIDC), and custom SLAs. Founded and led by Shvilesh (email: shvilesh@logen.io). The company is extremely early stage: the HN posting from July 2, 2026 mentions they are looking for their founding engineer. Stack mentioned includes Elixir/Phoenix, React, deployment infrastructure, and codegen systems. Team size is likely under 5 people given they are hiring their first founding engineer.
