# Company: SigNoz
- **Description**: SigNoz is a Bengaluru-headquartered, open-source observability platform that helps engineering teams monitor, debug, and optimize applications through unified metrics, traces, and logs. Built natively on OpenTelemetry (the CNCF standard for telemetry), it offers both self-hosted and cloud options, letting teams avoid vendor lock-in. The core product competes with Datadog and New Relic but is open-source and community-driven, with 28,000+ GitHub stars and 900+ paying customers. Revenue model is freemium SaaS: the open-source community edition is free, and SigNoz Cloud charges based on usage (data volume ingested). Backed by Y Combinator (W21) and prominent Bay Area VCs. The team has grown to ~38 people and is fully remote. Founded in 2021 by Pranay Prateek (former Microsoft, led product teams in biometrics/image recognition startups) and Ankit Nayan (distributed systems engineer with crypto background). Their recent push is agent-native observability: making AI agents first-class users of the platform, with an internal AI teammate called Noz that can query observability data, and a SigNoz MCP server that exposes metrics/traces/logs to external LLM agents. This is a significant strategic shift toward AI-native infrastructure.
