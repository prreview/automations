# Company: Edysor AI
- **Description**: Edysor AI is a Udaipur-based voice AI platform that deploys intelligent agents for enterprise lead conversion. Co-founded in January 2020 by Atishaya Jain, Abhinav Jain, Gunjan Pancholi, and Fivos Radis. The startup pivoted to AI in 2024 and now offers voice AI agents to enterprises across real estate, BPO, D2C, automotive, edtech, healthcare, and BFSI. The platform follows an outcome-based pricing model, charging businesses based on results rather than runtime. AI voice agents call potential customers within seconds of inquiry, conduct adaptive conversations, qualify prospects, handle objections, and move leads through the sales funnel. Also offers chat agents for 24/7 student engagement and instant AI-powered offers. Clients include Chanakya University, Madanapalle Institute of Technology, Global Tree, Ambuja, Rainmaker, Pyrotech, PU Sterling College. Claims 15 enterprise customers added in 2 months since launch, 300% MoM increase in voice minute consumption, 50K-100K AI calls per month for some clients, 15-20x ROI from AI-powered sales qualification. Just raised Rs 1.2 Cr in pre-seed funding led by angel investor Jasmine Sarupria. Plans to use funds for product development, AI capabilities, customer base expansion, team growth, and operations scaling. Team size is small (early-stage, likely 5-15 people).
