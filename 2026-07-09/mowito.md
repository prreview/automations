# Company: Mowito
- **Description**: Mowito is a Bengaluru-based robotics and physical AI startup building AI models that run standard industrial robot arms. Founded in 2024 by Puru Rastogi and Adityanag Nagesh, Mowito's physical AI enables robots to learn from operators to perform new tasks directly on the factory floor, without traditional coding methods. Their NeuralPick AI delivers fixture-free automation with 200-micron inspection and assembly accuracy, real-time adaptation, and operator-first control. Currently operates across Bengaluru and Detroit, supporting customers in automotive and electronics manufacturing. Claims robots deployed on manufacturing lines at a Fortune 500 automotive company and one of the world's largest electronics contract manufacturers. Just raised $3 Mn in pre-seed funding led by Version One Ventures, with participation from All In Capital, Unisol, iSeed. Angel investors include Thinking Machines Lab CTO Soumith Chintala, Foundry Robotics CEO Adarsh Kulkarni, Coformer.ai CEO Ashish Kulkarni, and Better Capital CEO Vaibhav Domkundwar. Plans to use funds to expand into the US, strengthen engineering and go-to-market teams, and advance physical AI platform. Team size is small (early-stage, likely 5-15 people). Contact emails: puru@mowito.ai, aditya@mowito.ai, careers@mowito.in.
