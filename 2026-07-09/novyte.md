# Company: Novyte Materials
- **Description**: Novyte Materials is a Mumbai-based deeptech startup using agentic AI to solve the optimization puzzle in materials science. Founded in 2025 by chemical engineer Ajaz Khan and incubated at the Institute of Chemical Technology (ICT), Mumbai. Novyte is building AI models that help chemical and materials companies optimize existing products, discover new formulations, and automate large parts of industrial R&D. Its flagship platform, Novyte Q, analyzes hundreds of research papers (500-1000 per iteration), suggests possible formulations, recommends experimental pathways, and uses reinforcement learning alongside density functional theory (DFT, a quantum chemistry technique) to evaluate whether proposed materials are chemically stable. The platform aims to shorten research timelines from years to months. Backed by Thiea Ventures. Revenue model: enterprise integration and research service. Most chemical manufacturers spend years refining existing formulations (changing ingredient concentrations, replacing hazardous chemicals, improving product performance, adapting for different production environments), and Novyte automates much of that work. Competitors: CuspAI (Cambridge, $400M term sheet at $2.6B valuation), Citrine Informatics, Orbital Materials. Market: global AI in Materials Discovery Market expected to reach $5.5B by 2034 (CAGR 26.4%). Team size is very early (likely under 10 people). Website (novyte.ai) is minimal.
