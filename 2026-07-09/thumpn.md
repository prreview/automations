# Company: thumpN
- **Description**: thumpN is India's first AI-native live entertainment discovery and ticketing platform, headquartered in Mumbai. Founded by ex-Paytm Insider COO Varun Khare, thumpN uses a conversational AI assistant called "Shadow" to recommend concerts, comedy shows, festivals, sports events, and nightlife experiences based on user preferences. The platform combines AI with a network of curators across cities and genres to surface relevant live events. Unlike traditional ticketing platforms (BookMyShow, District by Zomato, SkillBox), thumpN focuses on discovery first. For event organizers, it builds software tools that provide insights into audience preferences, event demand, genre trends, and consumer behavior. Revenue model: ticketing commission bundled with organizer tools (not sold as standalone paid products). Just launched on July 6, 2026. Raised $3.75M (₹35.7 Cr) in a pre-seed round from Vijay Shekhar Sharma's VSS Investco, Paytm CFO Madhur Deora, Hyperlink Brand Solutions' VG Jairam, Capitalmind cofounder Shray Chandra, and celebrity angel investors including Arijit Singh, Sunidhi Chauhan, and Badshah. Registered as Thumpn Entertainment Pvt. Ltd. (CIN: U90009MH2025PTC459468), based at WeWork Vaswani Chambers, Worli, Mumbai. Team size is early-stage, likely 10-25 people given the scope of open roles. Using Teamtailor as their ATS (hiring.thumpn.com).
