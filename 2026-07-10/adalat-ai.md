# Company: Adalat AI
- **Description**: Adalat AI is a Delhi NCR-based legal-tech startup building India's end-to-end justice tech stack. Founded in 2023 by Utkarsh Saxena and Arghya Bhattacharya (both MIT incubated), Adalat AI builds specialized AI models tailored for the Indian judicial system. Their product suite covers real-time transcription (15+ Indian languages, 90%+ accuracy), live case flow management for courts, LLM-based scanning and translation of court records, legal document intelligence, AI chatbots for litigants via WhatsApp, and AI-powered legal summarization. They serve active courtrooms with products that work on shaky 2G networks. Selected for Google for Startups Accelerator India 2026 cohort (one of 20 from ~2,500 applicants). Team size is small, remote-first within India. Revenue model is likely B2B government/enterprise contracts with courts and judicial departments. No public funding data available.
