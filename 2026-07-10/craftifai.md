# Company: CraftifAI
- **Description**: CraftifAI is a Bengaluru-based deep-tech startup building an agentic AI platform for embedded software development. Their product suite, CraftifAI Orbit (comprising PipeGen, FirmGen, and AgentIQ), automates firmware generation, embedded software development, and AI model deployment for edge, IoT, robotics, automotive, and industrial devices. The platform uses AI agents to handle the full lifecycle: from generating embedded code to deploying models on hardware like NVIDIA Jetson, Sima.ai, Rockchip, and others. Founded in 2024 by Pratik Sharda and Yashwant Dagar, the company raised $3M from Ankur Capital, IvyCap Ventures, Capital A, and Antler. They were also selected for the Google for Startups Accelerator India 2026 cohort (one of 20 from ~2,500 applicants), and are part of the NVIDIA Inception Program and Bosch partnerships. Their revenue model is B2B SaaS, selling the Orbit platform to engineering teams at manufacturing, robotics, and IoT companies. With the $3M raise and accelerator backing, runway is likely 18-24 months. The team is 11-50 people with offices in Bengaluru (HSR Layout), Sunnyvale CA, and Mainz, Germany.
