# Company: ProcIndex
- **Description**: ProcIndex is a full stack AI accounting firm building a suite of AI agents to automate quote-to-cash, accounts payable, accounts receivable, month-end close, and reconciliation processes end to end. The platform connects with existing ERPs (SAP S/4HANA, Oracle, NetSuite, Dynamics 365, QuickBooks, Sage, Epicor) and operates in the background while giving finance teams full visibility and control. The agents handle invoice capture, approval routing, vendor workflows, reconciliation, and continuous close, aiming for 95% straight-through processing without human touch. Founded in 2025 by Neha Suresh (ex-Zoho Books engineering lead, CMU alum) and Akash Thakur (ex-Apple). The company went through YC Summer 2025 and is based in San Francisco with an India team.

  Revenue model: B2B SaaS, likely per-seat or transaction-volume based pricing targeting mid-market finance teams. SOC 2 Type II certified. Product is live with paying customers.

  Funding: YC S25 backed. Total funding amount not publicly disclosed beyond YC's standard investment.
