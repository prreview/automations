# Company: Elimai.ai
- **Description**: Elimai is a Coimbatore company founded in 2025. It sells workflow agents for healthcare and manufacturing teams, working inside existing ERP, MES, PLM, accounting, and email systems. Its current product pages describe invoice and expense-document capture, duplicate-invoice checks, approval routing, GSTR-2B reconciliation, and email handling.
