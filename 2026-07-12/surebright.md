# Company: SureBright
- **Description**: SureBright is a New Delhi company, founded in 2022 and part of YC Summer 2024, with a 30-person team. It puts accidental, extended, whole-system, and shipping-protection plans into merchant checkout flows across e-commerce and POS channels. The merchant gets a share of every policy sold; SureBright handles plan setup, qualification, pricing, claims support, and the customer-facing purchase flow. Its site says more than 500 brands use the product and that its workflow covers 60+ product categories. YC says 5 million consumers see SureBright warranty offers each month. The company’s public materials say its LLM-based system matches products with policies and prices; its public site also mentions an API and integrations with major e-commerce and POS platforms. Funding is publicly confirmed through YC’s S24 batch. A funding amount, cash balance, revenue, and runway were not published, so they are not estimated here.
