# Company: Jiga
- **Description**: Jiga is a sourcing platform for engineers and procurement teams. It connects buyers with vetted manufacturers, handles quoting and communication in one place, and tracks orders from prototype to mass production. The company says it has 25 employees, is cashflow positive, and is growing revenue by 3x YoY. Funding is public too, a $12M Series A led by Aleph with participation from Symbol and Y Combinator. Runway is not public, but the team says it shares revenue, valuation, runway, and roadmap internally.
