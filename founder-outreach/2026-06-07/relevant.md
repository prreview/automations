# Company: Relevant Healthcare
- **Description**: Relevant is a healthcare analytics and population-health company for community health centers. The product helps clinics clean up EHR data, surface care gaps, run reports, and handle day-to-day analytics work, then backs that software with hands-on consulting and support. Public pages say the company was founded in 2015, is bootstrapped, profitable, and run by a 24-person team. More than 90 health centers use it, covering 10M+ patients. The business looks self-funded and cash-flow backed, with runway coming from revenue instead of outside capital. Main competitors are broader healthcare analytics vendors like Arcadia, Innovaccer, and Health Catalyst, plus more focused population-health tools. Relevant’s edge is that it stays close to community health centers and seems willing to do the unglamorous work with them.
