# Company: Sequent Tech
- **Description**: Sequent Tech builds open-source online voting software. The product is end-to-end verifiable, so voters can check that a ballot was cast and counted right, and auditors can check the result too. The site says Sequent has been used in 200+ real elections across multiple countries and has counted 2.7 million votes. It sells in a B2B, demo-led way, which points to contract and enterprise deals with election managers, public sector buyers, and private-sector election operators. Funding is public too: the LinkedIn page mentions a $1.35M pre-seed round led by RUNI Ventures and Tom Livne. Runway is not public, but the round and team size suggest they still have room to hire.
