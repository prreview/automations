# Company: CO-Ver
- **Description**: CO-Ver is a tiny, self-funded B2B SaaS for construction developers. It checks every change order against the original contract and regional market rates, then flags the lines that need a closer look. The contact page says the product runs on a subscription + project fee model, so revenue should grow with project volume instead of one big one-time sale. It’s built for the owner side of the table, with human estimators in the loop, Procore sync, and Autodesk support, which keeps the workflow close to the real mess instead of a demo. Public funding is not listed, and the HN post says the MVP is nearing completion, so they look lean and early.
