# Company: CoPlane
- **Description**: CoPlane is a seed-stage platform for enterprise back-office work. It sits on top of existing finance and ops systems, then uses agents, rules, and human review to handle things like source-to-pay, quote-to-cash, and record-to-report. The business model looks like enterprise software with implementation work around the edges, so revenue is likely contract-led rather than self-serve. They raised a $14M seed round led by Ribbit, with Stripe, Optum Ventures, and Terrain in the mix. Public runway is not listed, but the round and the customer-heavy sales motion suggest they have room to keep hiring.
