# Company: Lago
- **Description**: Lago builds open-source billing infrastructure for SaaS, AI, fintech, and IoT teams that need usage metering, invoices, credits, coupons, taxes, and pricing changes without rebuilding billing in-house. The revenue model is classic open-core: self-hosted open-source for adoption, then paid Cloud and enterprise plans for hosted billing, security needs, support, and larger teams. YC lists Lago as a Summer 2021 company with 45 team members and more than $22M raised. That funding level gives a decent runway for a 45-person team if burn is controlled, and the Jun 2026 hiring push points to active product and GTM buildout. Competitors include Stripe Billing, Orb, Metronome, Chargebee, and homegrown billing systems inside fast-growing SaaS companies.
