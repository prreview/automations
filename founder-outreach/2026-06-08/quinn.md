# Company: Quinn
- **Description**: Quinn is the AI scientist product from iollo, Inc., a Stanford spinout backed by Y Combinator S22. The product helps biotech and pharma teams answer drug program questions in 2 to 5 days: target validation, indication picks, biomarker strategy, trial design, safety review, and competitive checks. Quinn pulls together public evidence and customer data, then ships a traceable evidence package with reproducible code, documented methods, and a scientific briefing. The revenue model looks like enterprise services plus software around named drug assets; the website pushes a scoped 30-minute call and project deliverables instead of self-serve SaaS. Funding details beyond YC backing were not found in open sources during this run, so runway is best read from hiring intent, SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, HIPAA claims, Fortune 500 pharma usage, and the $170K to $270K remote engineering role. Main competitors include expert consulting shops, pharma data platforms, literature intelligence tools, and internal computational biology teams.
