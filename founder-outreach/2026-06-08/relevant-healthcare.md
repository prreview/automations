# Company: Relevant Healthcare
- **Description**: Relevant builds analytics software and hands-on data support for community health centers in the US. The product helps clinics plan visits, track clinical quality, monitor opioid prescribing, run immunization work, and turn EHR data into operational reports for care teams. Revenue is likely B2B SaaS plus services, sold to nonprofit health centers that need population health, data warehousing, ETL, and BI help. The company says it is bootstrapped and profitable, with no outside capital, so runway depends on customer revenue rather than fundraise timing. LinkedIn shows 23 employees, and the jobs page says the team has 24 people, which puts it inside the 5-300 filter. Competitors include Azara Healthcare, Arcadia, Health Catalyst, Innovaccer, and in-house EHR analytics teams.
