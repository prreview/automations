# Company: Roundly
- **Description**: Roundly is a focused interview prep product for new graduate nurses. The product lets a nurse pick a specialty panel, answer spoken clinical and behavioral scenarios, then get scored on clinical reasoning, SBAR or STAR structure, and delivery. The site says it is built with nurse recruiters and hiring managers, which gives the product a narrow wedge: new-grad RN residency interviews where one failed panel can delay a career by 6 months. Revenue likely comes from direct paid prep plans for nursing students and new nurses, with room for school, bootcamp, and hospital residency partnerships later. Funding is not public; the current signal looks bootstrapped or very early. Runway is unknown, but the hiring post asks for a part-time or full-time engineer, which fits a small product team adding core engineering capacity without a large hiring plan.
