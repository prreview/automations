# Company: DearHiringManager.io
- **Description**: DearHiringManager.io is an early-stage SaaS product from Karhuno Group that helps job seekers find the real hiring manager behind a role. A user pastes a job post URL, then the product returns the likely manager, work email, and LinkedIn. The product is live, has real users, and charges through a freemium model: 1 free lookup every 48 hours, paid packs at $5 for 10 lookups, $15 for 25 lookups, and a $19/month plan. The role maps well to Raj because the product needs job post parsing, contact enrichment, AI prompts, auth, payments, saved results, and production reliability. Raj has shipped Next.js/React codegen at WaveMaker, LLM workflows at Rava.ai, and Trevyn, a repo-aware AI PR reviewer.
