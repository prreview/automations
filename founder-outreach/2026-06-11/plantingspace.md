# Company: PlantingSpace
- **Description**: PlantingSpace AG is building an AI system that models domain knowledge as modular probabilistic models. The product is meant to answer natural language or domain-specific questions with source-backed reasoning steps and uncertainty attached to the answer. Their first named use cases are finance and scientific research, including equity valuation, distress monitoring, and particle physics. The commercial path looks like expert software for analysts and research teams, likely sold as B2B access plus domain work around high-value use cases. Public funding data was not found during this run. Runway is unknown, but the company says it is hiring across several dev and research tracks, runs a fully remote team across 14 countries, and gives team members a path to become shareholders from day one.
