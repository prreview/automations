## Company Profile
Puma.tech sits at the overlap of mobile browsing, local models, and crypto identity. Puma Browser is already public on iOS and Android, with 900k+ downloads stated on the site, so this is past toy stage.

The product bet is clear: keep more AI work on-device, let users bring model/API choices, and make the browser a place where agents can read, act, and pay without sending every private interaction to a server.

Competitors include Arc Search, Perplexity browser, Dia, Brave, Opera AI, and local-first tools like Jan or LM Studio. Puma is smaller, but the mobile plus wallet angle gives it a sharper wedge.

Raj matches well for the AI and browser-extension side. WaveMaker maps to code generation and production Next.js output, Trevyn maps to repo-aware AI tooling, and Rava maps to LLM workflows shipped for real users.
