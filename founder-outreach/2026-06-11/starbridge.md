### Company notes
Starbridge is building sales software for public-sector GTM teams. The pitch is concrete: public buying data is scattered across RFP portals, meeting notes, agency pages, education sites, vendor spend databases, and LinkedIn-type buyer movement. Starbridge bundles that into account scoring, decision-maker search, spend intelligence, RFP discovery, proposal writing, conference intelligence, and workflow tools.

The customer proof is decent. A June 10 LinkedIn post says Upciti cut government account research time by 75% using Starbridge. The website also points at case studies and testimonials from sales teams using the product to replace spreadsheet-heavy public-sector research.

The engineering fit is strong for Raj. Product Engineer maps to WaveMaker work on React, Next.js output, live preview-style product surfaces, and build-time component systems. AI Engineer maps to Trevyn and Rava: repo-context AI review, RAG workflows, document embeddings, LLM pipelines, and production reliability. Backend work maps to Rava's agents, tools, payments, billing, deployment, CI/CD, and API caching work.
