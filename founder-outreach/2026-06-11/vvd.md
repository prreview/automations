# Company: vvd
- **Description**: vvd is a browser workspace for people building fictional worlds, stories, campaigns, and public wikis. The product has an editor, canvas, relation trees, maps, shared wikis, custom domains, collaboration, and AI-assisted entity detection or suggestions. The public pricing page shows a freemium model: Free, Hobbyist at about $5.99/month billed yearly, and Pro at about $12.99/month billed yearly. That points to consumer SaaS revenue first, with team collaboration and custom domains as paid hooks. The company says 200,000+ creators use it, and the careers page lists 3 open roles, which fits an early team still hiring core product builders. Funding was not found in public sources during this run, so runway is unknown.
