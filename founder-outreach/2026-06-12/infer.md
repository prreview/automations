# Company: Infer
- **Description**: Infer builds AI agents for insurance agencies. The product handles work that agencies still do by hand: inbound lead qualification, live-call assistance for producers, call QA, renewal follow-ups, and win-back flows for churned customers. The wedge is agency ops, where call data, customer context, carrier rules, and producer workflow all meet. Revenue is likely B2B SaaS plus usage or deployment fees, since the product runs voice agents across live calls and connects into agency systems. Infer is YC S21, has raised about $6M from YC, Stellaris Venture Partners, Ahead VC, and others, and has a 9-person team. With that funding and headcount, runway looks healthy if burn stays lean, probably enough to hire a small India engineering pod while pushing US agency deployments. Competitors include voice-AI companies selling into SMB ops, insurance CRM vendors adding automation, and agency management systems that may add AI call workflows. The hard part is evals, latency, telephony, and compliance, which maps well to Raj's Trevyn and Rava work.
