# Company: LabAMI
- **Description**: LabAMI builds Coda, an iOS app for private music teachers and students. The product joins scheduling, lesson notes, practice recordings, tuner/metronome tools, teacher review, and student progress tracking in 1 loop. The company says it is a Delaware C-Corp, joined Microsoft for Startups Founders Hub and Google for Startups Cloud in 2024, shipped an MVP and iOS beta in 2025, and is onboarding educators and students through the 2026 academic year. Revenue is planned through teacher subscriptions with tiered student seats, educator partnerships, and paid content. Funding looks pre-seed or angel stage: the site says they are meeting angels and early-stage funds, with Microsoft and Google startup credits listed, but no priced round found.
