# Company: Rerun
- **Description**: Rerun is building the data stack for physical AI: logging, viewing, querying, storing, and streaming multimodal robotics data. The open-source Rerun SDK has 10.9k GitHub stars and is used by robotics and computer vision teams to inspect temporal sensor data, video, point clouds, tensors, annotations, and robot state. The paid product, Rerun Hub, is a cloud and customer-deployed data catalog/storage layer for robot training data, with early design partners and petabyte-scale workloads mentioned on the careers page. Revenue likely comes from hosted Hub usage, private deployments, and enterprise support for robotics and embodied AI teams. Rerun raised a $17M seed round led by Point Nine, with Costanoa, Sunflower Capital, Seedcamp, and angels including Guillermo Rauch, Eric Jang, Oliver Cameron, Wes McKinney, and Nicolas Dessaigne. With a small remote team and a recent seed round, runway looks healthy, probably 18-30 months depending on hiring pace.
