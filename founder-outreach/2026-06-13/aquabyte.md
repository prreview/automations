# Company: Aquabyte
- **Description**: Aquabyte builds AI and computer vision systems for fish farms. Its product combines underwater cameras, sonar, environmental sensors, cloud pipelines, and a farmer-facing web app to measure biomass, count lice, watch fish welfare, track behavior, guide feeding, and support short-term planning. The company was founded in 2017, opened its Bergen office in 2017, reached 50 people in 2022, and kept shipping new products through 2026, including HYDRA 360 in May 2026. Revenue likely comes from hardware plus recurring software and support sold to salmon and trout producers. Runway looks healthier than a lab-only startup because Aquabyte has fielded 800+ systems in pens, processes 1.3 million daily images per camera, and has direct farm customers across Norway, Chile, and other aquaculture markets.
