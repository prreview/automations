# Company: Emergences Labs
- **Description**: Emergences Labs is a small AI research and product company building NeoHuman, a desktop-first system for evaluating AI-native work. The product records how a candidate uses AI tools, files, apps, prompts, copy/paste, verification steps, and submissions during a real task, then converts that session into evidence for hiring, reskilling, expert review, and AI evaluation data. The business model looks B2B: sell assessment workflows to companies hiring AI-native roles, sell reskilling programs to teams, and build expert-curated workflow datasets for agent evaluation and training. Funding is not publicly detailed, but the HN post says the company is VC-backed. The team page shows 6 people, so burn is likely still founder-led and hiring is focused on one core engineering owner for the capture and evidence pipeline.
