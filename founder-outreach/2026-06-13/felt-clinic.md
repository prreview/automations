# Company: Felt Clinic
- **Description**: Felt Clinic builds clinical rails for consumer health apps. The product is an embedded care SDK plus APIs, provider access, HIPAA work, 50-state coverage, real-time communication, and care flow tooling that lets an app add doctor access inside its own product. The site says Felt has 2 live partners, 450K monthly users under care, under 5 minutes to a doctor, and 99.99% uptime. The revenue model looks like B2B platform fees tied to partner apps, care volume, and provider coverage. Funding is listed in the HN post as backing from 1984, Scrum Ventures, and Tau Ventures; no round size was found in the reachable sources. Runway cannot be verified from public data, but the HN post says 3 name-brand apps are using the SDKs and Felt expects about $10M ARR by late 2026. Likely competitors include Wheel, SteadyMD, OpenLoop, and Healthie, though Felt is aimed at product teams that want care inside their own app instead of a white-label clinic.
