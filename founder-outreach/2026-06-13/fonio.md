# Company: fonio.ai
- **Description**: fonio.ai is a Vienna company building AI phone assistants for European SMBs. The product answers calls, books appointments, handles FAQs, routes support requests, and can trigger outgoing calls through an API. Its own site says it builds its own orchestration layer in Europe instead of white-labelling US services, which matters for DACH buyers with data and language concerns. Revenue is subscription-led, with pricing starting at 85€ per month, plus call usage and add-ons around WhatsApp and customer workflows. The company says it was founded in 2024 by Daniel Keinrath and Matthias Gruber, acquired fluently in September 2025, raised a 3 million euro angel round in December 2025, and announced a 17 million dollar seed round led by 20VC on June 9, 2026 at a 140 million dollar valuation. The team page says 50 people today and a plan to double by year-end, so runway looks strong after the seed raise, though hiring speed is the main execution risk.
