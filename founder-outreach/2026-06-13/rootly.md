# Company: Rootly
- **Description**: Rootly is a YC S21 company building on-call, incident response, status page, retrospectives, and service catalog software for engineering and security teams. The product plugs into Slack, Microsoft Teams, PagerDuty, Jira, GitHub, Linear, Datadog, and cloud monitoring tools, then gives teams one place to declare incidents, assign owners, run timelines, write postmortems, and manage customer status updates. The revenue model is B2B SaaS with a free trial, paid tiers for startup and enterprise teams, and a “Pay What You Can” plan for teams under 25 people. YC lists Rootly as founded in 2020, active, based in San Francisco, with a team size of 75. YC also links Rootly’s funding news: a $3.2M seed in 2021 and a $12M round in August 2023.
