# Company: Booko
- **Description**: Booko is a YC W26 company building pricing software for businesses that sell bookable time: fitness studios, clinics, med-spas, tutors, consultants, and service chains. The product plugs into booking systems, reads historical use, live availability, and demand signals, then changes prices or incentives by slot. YC says early customers see about 20% revenue lift from filling time that would have gone unsold. The likely revenue model is SaaS plus a performance-linked layer for revenue lift, especially for chains where one pricing gain compounds across many locations. Funding is YC W26 by public batch signal; runway is probably seed-stage, small-team runway, with sales risk tied to booking-system integrations and trust in automated price changes. Competitors include legacy booking tools, revenue-management systems in hospitality, and vertical SaaS products that may bolt pricing onto scheduling.
