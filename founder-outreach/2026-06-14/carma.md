# Company: Carma
- **Description**: Carma builds the operating system for fleet operations. It sits on top of repairs, vendor selection, compliance, and dispatch, then turns messy fleet work into actions that get done fast. The customer base is enterprise fleets, including Fortune 500 operators, so the money likely comes from B2B contracts tied to fleet management and workflow automation. Carma raised a $5.5M seed from Y Combinator, Soma Capital, and the founders of Superhuman, and it says it is heading toward a Series A in 2026. Public runway is not disclosed, but a 15-person team and active hiring for 2 senior engineering roles suggest they have enough capital to keep pushing the product and get more accounts live.
