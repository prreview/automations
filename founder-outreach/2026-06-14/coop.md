# Company: Coop
- **Description**: Coop is an Austin consumer hardware startup selling automated chicken coops through smart.coop.farm. The product bundles WiFi cameras, automatic doors, solar power, predator detection, egg alerts, and remote health monitoring for backyard flocks. Revenue appears to come from direct hardware sales plus support and add-ons around the coop. AJ says the team is 5 people, the company has been in market about 18 months, serves customers in 48 states, and is on pace for more than 10M eggs this year. He also says Coop is VC-backed at about $5M. Public runway is not disclosed.
