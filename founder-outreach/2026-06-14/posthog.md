# Company: PostHog
- **Description**: PostHog is an open source product stack for engineers. The product now spans product analytics, web analytics, session replay, feature flags, experiments, surveys, error tracking, a data warehouse, CDP work, and LLM observability. The revenue model is usage based SaaS with a generous free tier, self serve signup, and larger paid accounts that need higher volume, compliance, or support. PostHog raised $70M in primary capital in June 2026 at a $920M valuation, led by Stripe, with YC, GV, and Formus Capital participating. That gives them room to hire across product teams while still keeping a small team model inside a 200 plus person company.
