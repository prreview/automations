# Company: Roundly
- **Description**: Roundly is a nurse interview prep product for new graduate RNs. The product lets a candidate pick a specialty, practice spoken panel questions, and get scored on clinical reasoning, SBAR/STAR structure, and delivery. The site is aimed at NCLEX and residency cohorts, with specialty flows for ER, ICU, L&D, Med-Surg, OR, Peds, NICU, and other units. The likely revenue model is self-serve subscription prep for candidates, with room for cohort or school partnerships later. Funding is not public from the sources checked, so I would treat this as bootstrapped or pre-seed until the founder says otherwise. Runway is unknown. Competitors include Big Interview, Interviewing.io-style mock interview tools, nursing school prep resources, and broad AI interview coaches. Roundly’s wedge is narrow and practical: panels for nurses, scored against the way nurse recruiters and hiring managers judge answers.
