# Company: Voiceops
- **Description**: Voiceops turns calls, email, SMS, and chat into structured customer intelligence. The product ingests past interactions, maps products, funnels, intents, and risks, then pushes that signal into CRM, BI, and internal tools so sales, CX, product, and ops work from the same facts. The company says it serves multiple $1B+ revenue companies, including Fortune 500 names, and it raised a $5M seed round co-led by Bonfire Ventures and Twelve Below, with support from Village Global, Precursor, Not Boring Capital, and operators from Anthropic and Airbnb. Revenue looks like B2B SaaS with enterprise rollout work on top. Runway is not public, but the company is active, has 10 people on the team, and is still adding engineers.
