# Company: Cardog
- **Description**: Cardog builds automotive intelligence software. The product splits into 3 parts: car search for buyers, tools for sellers, and APIs for developers. The company says it started in 2023 with VIN decoding, launched real-time market data and AI search in 2024, added dealer tools, auction intelligence, and enterprise APIs in 2025, and is still growing in 2026.
