# Company: Clad
- **Description**: Clad is a YC W23 B2B SaaS company for contractors and infrastructure operators. The product handles project ops and finance, and the newer Clad Locates product automates 811 ticket submission and tracking. The YC job page says the team is 7 people, the company raised $2.7M from Y Combinator, Better Tomorrow Ventures, and others, and it still has more than 5 years of runway with most of the money still in the bank. Public pricing is not listed, but this looks like annual software contracts for construction and infrastructure teams, plus onboarding for larger accounts.
