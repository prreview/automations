# Company: Evertune
- **Description**: Evertune is a b2b AI visibility platform for brand discovery in AI search. It tracks prompt volume, citations, and brand mentions across ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity, Copilot, Meta AI, and DeepSeek, then turns that into GEO, content, site, and ad work for marketing teams. Public company info says Evertune was founded in 2024 by people who helped build The Trade Desk, it has raised $20M from adtech and martech investors, and public size listings put it at 11-50 employees. That looks like a live business with customers, a demo-led revenue model, and room to keep hiring, though burn data is not public.
