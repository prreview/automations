# Company: Harmony AI
- **Description**: Harmony AI is a 2025 startup building an AI-native operating system for manufacturing. The product ties together machines, software, paperwork, and the tribal knowledge on a plant floor, then uses that data to give factories live visibility, auto-generate reporting, and ship workflows that fit inside the plant instead of forcing a rip-and-replace setup. Public pages point to a sales-led B2B model with deployments, plant visits, and customer-specific rollout work. I do not see a public funding round, but LinkedIn says the company is backed by founders from HubSpot, Typeform, Apollo, Hustle Fund, Brickyard, and others, so I’d treat it as early, angel-backed, and still figuring out exact runway.
