# Company: Neocom.ai
- **Description**: Neocom.ai builds shop agents for online retailers. The product is a guided-selling layer that lets shoppers chat naturally, get product advice, and find the right item faster. The company says it has been in guided selling for 6 years, launched the Shop Agent in November, and is already seeing customers ask for more features. The business looks B2B SaaS, sold to retailers that want a better on-site shopping flow and cleaner customer signals from zero-party data. I did not find a public funding announcement, so funding and runway are not public.
