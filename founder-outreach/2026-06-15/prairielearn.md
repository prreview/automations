# Company: PrairieLearn
- **Description**: PrairieLearn is an open-source assessment platform for universities. The product lets instructors build large banks of code-backed questions, give instant feedback, and run mastery-based courses at scale. The business model is plain: free for instructors, free for courses up to 20 students, then paid plans at $8 per student per course or $16 per student per course, plus custom enterprise pricing. The public footprint says the company is bootstrapped, has no VC, and is still early, so the real runway signal looks tied to product revenue and careful overhead rather than a fundraise clock.
