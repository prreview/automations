# Company: Shepherd
- **Description**: Shepherd builds insurance for the physical layer of AI. The company writes coverage for construction work tied to data centers, semiconductor fabs, solar, battery storage, and other high-hazard projects, then uses software to pull submissions, route underwriting work, and cut the back-and-forth that still lives in email and spreadsheets. In March 2026, Shepherd raised a $42M Series B, bringing total funding to $67M, and the hiring post says the engineering team is moving from 25 to 40. Revenue is insurance revenue, so the product sits on top of a real underwriting business, not a thin SaaS layer. Runway is not public, but the funding base looks solid.
