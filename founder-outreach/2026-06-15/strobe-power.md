# Company: Strobe Power
- **Description**: Strobe Power builds software for distributed energy sites. The product turns batteries, solar, generators, EV chargers, and HVAC into one site-level power plant, then sizes, simulates, dispatches, and settles it against utility tariffs and wholesale prices. The site says the controller is installed free, then Strobe shares in the savings it creates, so the business looks like a savings-share model with market revenue on top. Public funding details are thin, but Keeley says the team is well-funded and backed by top investors. The team is small, 7 people on LinkedIn and 6 in the HN post. Runway is not public.
