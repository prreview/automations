# Company: Veedma
- **Description**: Veedma is a cash-pay telehealth practice for men's hormonal and sexual health. It treats low testosterone, erectile dysfunction, libido, energy, strength, and mood, and it pushes natural testosterone first with enclomiphene, then tadalafil when needed. Revenue looks like direct subscription income, with an all-in plan at $99/month that includes physician consults, lab panels twice a year, compounded medication shipping, and unlimited messaging.

The team is small, 2-10 people on LinkedIn, and the company says it is founder-led with a remote US team and San Francisco HQ. It launched in 2025 and I did not find any public funding round, so runway is not public.
