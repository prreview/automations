# Company: Xata
- **Description**: Xata builds Postgres for agentic workloads. The product creates instant database branches, so every agent, workflow, or CI job can run against production-like data without copying full databases around. It is open source at the core, with hosted branching, storage, and platform tooling on top. The company says it has raised $35M and has years of runway, and LinkedIn shows 17 employees, so this is a small team with enough fuel to keep hiring. Revenue likely comes from the hosted platform, support, and enterprise use around its open-source Postgres tooling. The stack on the careers page is Go, Rust, PostgreSQL, Kubernetes, AWS, and GCP. The closest neighbors are Neon, Supabase, and PlanetScale, but Xata’s angle is tighter: safe branching for agent-era databases.
