# Company: L2 Labs
- **Description**: L2 Labs builds AI systems for EDI, APIs, B2B integrations, partner onboarding, and enterprise data translation. Their main product, Alloy, sits inside trading-partner onboarding and mapping work, where it reads implementation guides, raw EDI messages, and internal models, then drafts mappings for human review. A public case study with TrueCommerce says Alloy cut partner mapping setup time by 74.5% and projected $265,150 in yearly operating savings. The company says it closed an $875K pre-seed round with the Jacobs Institute at Cornell Tech and Focal VC, so this is still early and close to the customer.
