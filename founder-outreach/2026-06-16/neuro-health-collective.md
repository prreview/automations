# Company: Neuro Health Collective
- **Description**: Neuro Health Collective is building open infrastructure for neurological data. The public site says brain data should stay useful across institutions and stay readable by the AI tools that will shape diagnosis and treatment. Their compression tech is already in production at research and clinical neurology institutions, so this is not a sketch on a slide deck. The revenue shape looks like institutional software and data infrastructure sales to clinics, hospitals, and research groups. They do not publish pricing, but the site says they have $30M raised across pre-seed and seed, with support from Aleph, Deep33 Ventures, Standard Industries, XTX Markets, KDM Partners, Yasmin Lukatz, 202 Ventures, and Clocktower Ventures. That gives them real room to hire the first engineering layer and keep the lights on while the product matures.
