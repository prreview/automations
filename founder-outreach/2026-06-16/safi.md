# Company: Safi AI
- **Description**: Safi AI builds computer-vision quality checks for PET, HDPE, and UBC recyclers. The product takes a bale photo and returns composition analysis, which is aimed at the day-to-day QC flow on a plant floor. Their own site says pilots start with a billed setup and can run on-site or remotely, so this looks like a sales-led product with implementation work wrapped around the software. The LinkedIn company page shows 2-10 employees and the site says the company was founded in 2021 in London. I could not find a public funding round, so runway is not public. Competition looks like manual bale-break sampling, spreadsheet-based QC, and general machine-vision vendors trying to sell into recycling.
