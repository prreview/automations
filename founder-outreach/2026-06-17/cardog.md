# Company: Cardog
- **Description**: Cardog builds automotive intelligence software for cars, listings, recalls, charging, and VIN data. The public product has 3 layers: a consumer app, a developer API, and paid pricing tiers. The API page shows VIN decode, market intelligence, listings search, recalls, and EV charging data, with a starter plan at $23/month and 1,000 API calls, so the business looks like a mix of self-serve subscription and usage-based API revenue. Their about page says they launched in 2024, added dealer tools and enterprise APIs in 2025, and are still growing the team in 2026. Funding is not public on the site, but the page copy points to live customers, open pricing, and a company that is already selling.
