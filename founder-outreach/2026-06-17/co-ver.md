# Company: CO-Ver
- **Description**: CO-Ver is building software for construction developers to review and challenge change orders before they pay them. The product compares each line item against the original contract and live regional market pricing, then flags gaps that would otherwise slip through spreadsheets and email threads. It sits on the owner and developer side of construction, which is a good wedge because that workflow still runs on manual review in a lot of places. The June 4 HN post says the company is self-funded, intentionally small, and aiming at production in mid-July, and the public LinkedIn page shows 2-10 employees. The revenue model is not public, but this looks like a direct B2B sale to developers and owners, likely with software fees tied to projects or seats. Burn should be low because the team is tiny and self-funded, so runway looks lean rather than loose.
