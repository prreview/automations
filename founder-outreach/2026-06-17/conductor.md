# Company: Conductor
- **Description**: Conductor is a Mac app for running coding agents in parallel. It clones a repo into isolated workspaces, runs Claude Code, Codex, and Cursor side by side, then shows the diff, preview, and checks in one place. The company also points at Conductor Cloud and an enterprise motion, so the revenue path looks like SaaS plus enterprise deals. Publicly they say they have grown 10x since January and raised $24M in a Series A. Runway is not public, but that raise and the small team on the join page suggest they have room to hire.
