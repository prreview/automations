# Company: Memora ApS
- **Description**: Memora is grave-care software for cemeteries, gardeners, and family-facing operations. The public site says 20+ cemeteries already use it, and the product is built so families can see what’s happening while the people on the ground can do their work with less manual back-and-forth. The careers page says the team is deliberately lean, backed by investors, and hiring from a stack that includes NestJS, TypeScript, PostgreSQL + PostGIS, Inngest, React, TanStack Query, MapLibre, React Native + Expo, AWS, Terraform, Grafana, and Sentry. I’d treat this as B2B/B2G SaaS sold to cemeteries or municipalities. Exact pricing and runway are not public.
