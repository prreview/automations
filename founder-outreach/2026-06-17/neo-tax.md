# Company: Neo.Tax
- **Description**: Neo.Tax builds enterprise tax software that turns R&D credit and software capitalization work into something a finance team can actually run every month. The product pulls in Jira, Linear, Workday, payroll, ledgers, Confluence, Oracle NetSuite, GitHub, ADP, and Azure data, then turns that mess into audit-ready outputs for the R&D credit and ASC 350-40. Their revenue model looks like enterprise SaaS with high-touch onboarding and demo-led sales, since the site pushes walkthroughs and customer proof points rather than self-serve checkout. Funding is private and not fully disclosed, but the company says it is backed by operators who have built before. Runway is not public, though the LinkedIn company page shows 30 employees and the team is still hiring.
