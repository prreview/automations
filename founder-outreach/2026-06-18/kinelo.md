# Company: Kinelo
- **Description**: Kinelo is a 2023 San Francisco startup with 2-10 employees on LinkedIn. It builds software for hybrid teams, meaning humans and AI coworkers working from the same source of truth. The public site splits the product into Company Brain, AI Management System, and Shared Work Surfaces. That points to B2B software sold to engineering and product teams, with later room for broader team operations. The product sits across Slack, Linear, GitHub, Notion, Google Drive, Google Meet, Gmail, Jira, Confluence, Figma, Salesforce, HubSpot, Zendesk, and Asana, so the wedge is context, handoffs, and memory across tools. Revenue likely comes from team subscriptions and enterprise deals, though pricing is not public. I could not find a public funding round, so the money side is still private. Runway is not public either, but the team is live, shipping, and hiring, which says they still have room to build the core product.
