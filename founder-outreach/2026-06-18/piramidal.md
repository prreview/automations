# Company: Piramidal
- **Description**: Piramidal is building a brain foundation model around EEG and other neural signals. Their first product is a copilot for epilepsy diagnosis, and the broader plan is to use the same core model for neurological diagnosis, sleep and cognitive disorders, drug and biomarker discovery, and remote brain monitoring. Public revenue details are thin, but the shape looks B2B, with clinical software and research partnerships around EEG data; they also say research access is free while the product is being built. The company is YC Winter 2024, has 10 people, and is backed by a $6M seed round led by Y Combinator, Lionheart Ventures, and Adverb VC. Public hiring points to a research side and a UI side, so the stack looks like Python / ML on one side and React / TypeScript on the other, but they haven't published a full stack doc.
