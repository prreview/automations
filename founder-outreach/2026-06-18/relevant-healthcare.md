# Company: Relevant Healthcare
- **Description**: Relevant is a 24-person, bootstrapped healthcare software company founded in 2015. It sells a data platform for community health centers and pairs that with hands-on data consulting, so revenue comes from software plus services. The company says it is profitable, has no outside capital, and serves 90+ health centers across the U.S. The stack is TypeScript, React, Apollo, d3, Rails, Go, and GraphQL. Runway looks steady because they keep the pace measured instead of chasing a big burn.
