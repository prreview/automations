# Company: Arzana
- **Description**: Arzana is a 5-person YC-backed startup building an autonomous ERP for manufacturers. Their public pitch is simple: train custom models on a factory's historical data, then use agents to handle quoting, order entry, invoicing, and customer updates. The site points to a sales-led B2B motion, with a clear "book a demo" path and claims that customers have already saved more than $1M in avoided staff costs. Public stack notes: Next.js on the front end, Python/Flask on Railway, Supabase/Postgres, and AI services from Gemini, Anthropic, OpenAI, Perplexity, Exa, plus Claude Code. Funding beyond Y Combinator is not public, and runway isn't public either, but the current hiring pattern reads like an early seed-stage team still building hard.
