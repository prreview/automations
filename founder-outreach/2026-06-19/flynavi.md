# Company: Navi AI
- **Description**: Navi AI builds software for pilot training. It takes cockpit audio, aircraft telemetry, and flight data, then turns each flight into a debrief for instructors and trainees. The company says it is live at Embry-Riddle, Purdue, UND, Sling Pilot Academy, and the U.S. Air Force.

  It raised more than $6.7M total: $1.5M pre-seed in 2024, $3.35M seed in March 2026, and a $1.27M SBIR grant from the U.S. Department of War. The round includes United Airlines Ventures, BVVC, New Vista Capital, Raptor Group, and I2BF. It has 7 people and plans to add 15 in 2026, so it is still small and hiring.

  The company looks like a B2B software sale to flight schools, universities, and defense teams. A public runway number is not out there, but the headcount and funding point to a lean 2026 build plan.
