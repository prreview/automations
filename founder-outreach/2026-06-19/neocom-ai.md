# Company: Neocom
- **Description**: Neocom is a Munich-based AI-native commerce company. Their site says the product is a "shop agent" for online stores, plus customer data and activation, shopping quiz, and product guidance flows. The public case studies point to retailers like mömax and emma & noah, so this looks like a B2B SaaS sale into e-commerce teams, with demo-led deals and setup work around the core product. I could not find a public funding round in indexed sources, and the runway is not public. The clearest public size signal I found is the HN hiring post, which says the engineering team is 4 people with 2 more starting in September, so the team is small and still building.
