# Company: Olli Health
- **Description**: Olli Health is a home-health coding and quality review company. It sells an AI-enabled managed service to home-health agencies for ICD-10 coding, OASIS review, and plan-of-care review, so clinicians spend less time buried in chart work and agencies get faster turnaround with fewer errors.

  LinkedIn lists 25 employees, 2,138 followers, and a 2023 founding date. The company is based in Madison, Wisconsin, and the public team page shows three co-founders plus a lean layer around quality, sales, and operations.

  The business looks like B2B service revenue, with agencies paying for coding and review work plus the software behind it. The live role pages say Olli Health is Series A funded, but I could not find a public round size or runway number.
