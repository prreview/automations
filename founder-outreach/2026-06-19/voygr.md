# Company: VOYGR
- **Description**: VOYGR builds a location and POI data layer for AI apps, analytics teams, and mapping products. The site says it turns address, contact, hours, menu, and price data into verified outputs, and the company talks about stale place data that breaks agent queries. It sells custom data work through a request-a-demo motion, with partner SLAs and revenue share on the contributor side. Public funding detail is thin, but VOYGR says it is backed by Y Combinator and top investors, has real revenue and enterprise contracts, and has multi-year runway. LinkedIn shows 6 employees, so this is still a small team.
