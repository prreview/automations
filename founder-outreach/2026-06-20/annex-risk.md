# Company: Annex Risk
- **Description**: Annex Risk is a 13-person insurtech that sells homeowners and flood coverage in hard-to-insure coastal markets. The product is built for retail agents, with quote-and-bind flows that finish in under 2 minutes and let agents place E&S coverage for homes in states like Florida, Texas, New Jersey, Georgia, and California. The business model looks like B2B2C insurance distribution, with revenue tied to policies placed through agents and the underwriting economics behind those placements. Competitors sit in the admitted homeowners market, the E&S specialty market, and other MGA-style platforms that serve high-risk housing. Public funding details are thin, but the company is live, expanding state coverage, and posting product and engineering hires in June 2026. Runway is not public.
