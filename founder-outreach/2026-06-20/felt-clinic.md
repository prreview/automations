# Company: Felt
- **Description**: Felt is a 2023, 8-person startup building clinical infrastructure for consumer apps. Their product is a single SDK plus a licensed clinical network, so an app can add doctor access, messaging, scheduling, labs, and pharmacy without building that stack itself. The business looks B2B, with platform fees plus hands-on implementation work, and the careers page says 3 name-brand apps already use the SDKs and that they are tracking toward roughly $10M ARR by late 2026. They are backed by 1984 and Scrum Ventures. Runway is not public.
- **Website**: https://feltclinic.com
- **Founders/CTO**: Not found publicly. The public LinkedIn company page lists McKay Thomas, Joshua Tolman, Sid Ghodke, and Rachel Steed as employees, but their titles are not public.
