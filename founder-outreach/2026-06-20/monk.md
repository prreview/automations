# Company: Monk
- **Description**: Monk is an AI-native accounts receivable platform. It helps finance teams run intelligent collections, invoice generation, cash application, and cash projection from one place, with native integrations into Stripe, HubSpot, QuickBooks, NetSuite, and Salesforce. The public pitch is simple: cut DSO, speed up collections, and take manual AR work off finance teams. Monk raised a $25M Series A on 2026-04-21, co-led by Footwork and Acrew, with Better Tomorrow Ventures back from seed. LinkedIn lists 11-50 employees and the company page shows 29 employees, so this is still a tight team. Revenue likely comes from annual SaaS contracts sold with a demo-led motion, and the site’s compare pages put Monk up against Billtrust, Esker, Tesorio, Sequence, and Invoice Butler. Runway looks decent after the Series A, but I couldn’t verify burn or exact months left from public pages.
