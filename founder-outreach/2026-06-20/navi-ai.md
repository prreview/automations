# Company: Navi AI
- **Description**: Navi AI builds cockpit debrief software for flight training. It listens to cockpit audio, pulls avionics and ADS-B data, then turns each sortie into a structured debrief for pilots and instructors. The public launch says the model has 100,000+ flight hours in it, is live at Embry-Riddle, Purdue, UND, Sling Pilot Academy, and the U.S. Air Force, and has moved past research into production use. Public pricing is not listed, but the buyer set looks like flight schools, universities, airlines, and defense teams, so this is probably sold through B2B contracts and site-level deployments. Funding is public too: $6.7M total, including a $1.5M pre-seed in Apr 2024, a $3.35M seed in Mar 2026, and a $1.27M SBIR grant. With 7 employees and a fresh seed round, they look built for a lean year of shipping, though exact runway is not public.
