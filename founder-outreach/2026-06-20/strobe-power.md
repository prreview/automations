# Company: Strobe Power
- **Description**: Strobe Power is a Brooklyn energy software company that runs distributed assets like batteries, generators, solar, EV chargers, and HVAC as one fleet. It sits between a building and the grid, then decides when to charge, discharge, or stay idle based on utility programs, demand charges, and wholesale prices. The money model is simple: the controller is deployed free, then Strobe shares the savings and market value it creates. LinkedIn lists 7 employees, and the company says FDVC has backed it from day one. I could not find a public round size or runway number. The product lives in the same lane as Voltus, CPower, EnergyHub, and GridBeyond.
