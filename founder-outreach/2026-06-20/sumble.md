# Company: Sumble
- **Description**: Sumble is a knowledge graph for go-to-market teams. It pulls job posts, resume data, and company sites into a graph that lets users query org structure, tech stacks, projects, and the people tied to those projects. The launch post says the product already has PMF, early revenue, and happy customers. The money path looks like free access for most users, with paid bulk exports for power users. Public funding is $38.5M from Coatue, Canaan, and Square Peg. The team is 15 people, with 10 engineers, so this is still a tight group. Runway is not public.
