# Company: Branch 3D
- **Description**: Branch 3D builds real-time structural design and fabrication software for AEC teams. Branch Concept lets architects and engineers compare concrete, steel, timber, and hybrid schemes in the browser, with live quantities, carbon, and cost updates, and Branch Detailing has already been used on 900,000+ square feet of built work inside StructureCraft. The business looks like software sold to design and fabrication teams, plus internal use inside StructureCraft, with no public VC round listed.
