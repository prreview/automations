# Company: Confident AI
- **Description**: Confident AI is a 7-person YC W25 company in San Francisco building an LLM eval and observability product around DeepEval, their open-source framework with 12.6k GitHub stars and 3m+ monthly downloads. The company sells a freemium SaaS layer for individual users, then moves teams into paid Starter and Premium plans, with Team and Enterprise priced custom and able to run in VPC or on-prem. Their site says pricing starts at $0/month, self-serve plans are $19.99 and $49.99 per user, and the enterprise path adds SOC 2, invoice billing, and self-hosted deployment. Runway isn’t public, and with YC backing plus 7 people, they look lean.
