# Company: Coulomb AI
- **Description**: Coulomb AI builds a real-time battery analytics layer for electric vehicles. Their site says it watches battery health, spots failure and downtime patterns, and sends corrective actions before packs fail. They sell to battery manufacturers, battery-as-a-service teams, automotive OEMs, and fleets, so the business looks like B2B software with enterprise deals and setup work.
