# Company: Guac
- **Description**: Guac is a 7-person YC-backed startup in New York that builds AI demand forecasting and replenishment software for grocery retailers. The product predicts order quantities for each item, each day, and each store location, then turns those forecasts into order suggestions for the people who buy inventory. The company says it reduces food waste and empty shelves, and its site shows a demo-led B2B motion, so this looks like software sold on contract to grocers rather than a consumer app. Guac raised a $2.3M seed round led by 1984 Ventures with participation from Y Combinator and Collaborative Fund. Public runway is not disclosed, but a 7-person team plus a seed round points to an early build-and-sell stage.
