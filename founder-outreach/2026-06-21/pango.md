# Company: Pango
- **Description**: Pango is a YC S26 startup building an agentic operating system for e-commerce logistics. The product pulls shipping, tracking, returns, delivery promise, and integrations into one system, and the site says it can automate most of the post-purchase grind for merchants. Their model is sales-led SaaS with custom pricing, and the careers page points to salary plus equity with a clear leveling setup. They say they spoke with 100+ merchants before writing code, secured customers willing to pay, and are already live with brands, so this looks like a company with real demand and early revenue. YC backing is public, but exact runway is not.
