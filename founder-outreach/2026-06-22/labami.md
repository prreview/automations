# Company: LabAMI
- **Description**: LabAMI is building an iOS-first practice app for music teachers and students. The site describes it as a sketchbook for music, with AI tools that connect both sides between lessons, and the product loop is built around practice notes, audio, and teacher feedback. Revenue comes from monthly subscriptions for teachers with tiered student seats, educator partnerships, and community content monetization. The site says the MVP shipped in mid-2025, the iOS beta is live, and they are actively meeting angels and early-stage funds. Public headcount and runway are not listed.
