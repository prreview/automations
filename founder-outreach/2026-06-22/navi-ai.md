# Company: Navi AI
- **Description**: Navi AI builds flight debrief software for pilot training. It pulls cockpit audio, aircraft telemetry, ATC, weather, and lesson context into automated debriefs, safety flags, and coaching for flight schools and military training. Public materials say it raised $6.7M total, including a $1.5M pre-seed in 2024, a $3.35M seed in March 2026, and a $1.27M SBIR Phase II contract from the U.S. Department of War. The team is 7 people in San Francisco and says it will add 15 more in 2026. Revenue looks like B2B software plus deployment and defense work. Runway is not public.
