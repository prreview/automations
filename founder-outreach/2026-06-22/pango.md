# Company: Pango
- **Description**: Pango is an ai-powered e-commerce logistics platform. It handles delivery management, multi-carrier routing, tracking, returns, exchanges, checkout delivery dates, and pick & pack, so it sits in the middle of the merchant-to-carrier workflow. The site says the team spoke with 100+ merchants before building, and it frames the product as one operating layer for shipping and logistics work. It was founded in 2023, is backed by Y Combinator, and the LinkedIn company page shows 17 employees, which keeps it inside the 5-30 filter. Revenue looks b2b saas or platform-based, sold via demo and sales-led motion. Exact burn and runway are not public, but a 17-person team with yc backing gives the company room to keep building. Main competitors: aftership, narvar, loop returns, shipbob.
